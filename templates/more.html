About Jitwarpur Village

Welcome to Jitwarpur

A vibrant rural community in Bihar, India with a rich cultural heritage, resilient people, and a strong tradition of craftsmanship. Home to 3,200+ residents, Jitwarpur is a model of sustainable village development through community participation and government schemes.

Local Artisans & Crafts

Jitwarpur is renowned for its traditional artisans who preserve centuries-old crafts. Their work supports local economy and cultural identity.

Pottery

Pottery Makers

45+ families craft eco-friendly clay pots, diyas, and decorative items. Annual turnover: ₹8.2 lakh

Weaving

Handloom Weavers

32 women produce Madhubani sarees and stoles. Linked with e-NAM & GeM for online sales.

Bamboo

Bamboo Craftsmen

28 artisans make furniture, baskets, and eco-products. Export to Patna & Delhi via KVIC.
