Jitwarpur: Gateway to Mithila Art

Welcome to the digital dashboard for Jitwarpur, Madhubani, Bihar. This village is celebrated worldwide as a cradle of Mithila Painting

b>, also known as Madhubani Art. It is a vibrant cultural hub where tradition meets technology to drive rural development.

The Specialty: Madhubani Painting

  • Artistic Heritage: Madhubani painting, traditionally done by women, uses natural dyes and pigments to depict religious themes, nature, and geometric patterns. Jitwarpur has produced many National Award-winning artists.
  • Economic Impact: The art sustains a large portion of the village economy. This dashboard supports local artisans through project visibility and complaint redressal.
  • Cultural Focus: We aim to preserve and promote this art by digitizing records and streamlining administrative processes for local craftspeople.
